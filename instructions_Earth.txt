## COORDINATE GUIDELINES
- lat: -90 to 90 (South to North)
- lng: -180 to 180 (West to East)
- zoom levels:
  * 1-3: Global/continental
  * 4-6: Regional (multiple states/provinces)
  * 7-9: Local (city/county level)
  * 10: Very detailed (neighborhood)
